Heli (헬리, Helli)

He is one of the seven main male protagonists of the original story DARK MOON: THE BLOOD ALTAR.
He is a vampire, student in the twelfth grade at Decelis Academy and the captain and center of the school Nightball Team.

Appearance

Heli has short raven black hair and yellow gold eyes. He wears a Decelis Academy uniform during standard school days. On nightball practice, games and sport competition he wears the vesselsoft uniform.

Powers and Abilities

•Vampire Physiology: As a vampire, Heli possesses superhuman agility, durability, senses, strength, speed, stamina and reflexes, also claw retraction, blood consumption, immortality, fang retraction and psychic communication.
•Telepathy: Heli can read and sense the thoughts of humans and vampires, also he is able to open a psionic communication line with them.
•Telekinesis: Heli can move items and objects around him according to his will.
•Sword Mastery
