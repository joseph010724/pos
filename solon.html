Solon (솔론)

He is one of the main characters of Dark Moon: The Blood Altar.
He is a vampire-werewolf hybrid in the tenth grade at Decelis Academy and a forward of the Nightball Team.

Personality

Solon is calm, intelligent and seems to be confident, but he believes that he's fundamentally different from his brothers. He tries to act cool and always tries to hide it.

Appearance

Solon has dark blue hair that fades to a turquoise with two odd-colored eyes, his left eye being blue and right eye being yellow. He has a slim body with broad shoulders and sharp features. On his left ear, he has a dangling earring and a studded earring. Once in contact with blood, his eyes begin to glow red with his fangs and claws extending. Under the moonlight, his eyes glow bright and his claws extend while turning black in color, and blue fur also begins to grow across his hands and neck.

History

Solon grew up in an 1800s orphanage and was there for a century with his 6 brothers and orphanage principal, Marge.

Powers and Abilities

• Vampire Physiology: As a Vampire, Solon possesses superhuman agility, durability, strength, speed, stamina and reflexes, also immortality and psychic communication.
• Superhuman Agility: As a Vampire, Solon has a heightened level of agillity in his movements.
• Superhuman Durability: As a Vampire, Solon has heightened amounts of durability.
• Superhuman Strength: As a Vampire, Solon is incredibly strong.
• Superhuman Speed: As a Vampire, Solon posesses heightened levels of speed.
• Superhuman Stamina: As a Vampire, Solon possesses an increased stamina.
• Superhuman Reflexes: As a Vampire, Solon has superhuman reflexes.
• Immortality: As a Vampire, Solon is genetically able to live forever, without dying due to old age or illnesses.
• Psychic Communication.
