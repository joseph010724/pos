TITLE:

7 FATES: CHAKHO

Is a original story produced by HYBE starring BTS. The original story began with a webtoon and a web novel published simultaneous since January 14, 2022.

This is the first story in the 7 FATES series.

PLOT:

The 7Fates: CHAKHO is an urban fantasy genre story set in a city of the near future. It was inspired by the "Chakhogapsa", the Beom (tiger) hunters of the Joseon Dynasty. It is a story re-created by reinterpreting the stories of bears and Beom in mythology and the traditional Korean beom tales with HYBE's own imagination. CHAKHO will collaborate with BTS to create a dynamic story about seven boys bound by fate who grow up together through trials and tribulations, and why they must be seven and what their destiny holds.



TITLE:

Crimson Heart (크림슨 하트; Keulimseun hateu)

Is an original story produced by HYBE starring LE SSERAFIM.

The original story began with CRIMSON HEART : PROLOGUE book released on October 17, 2022.

PLOT:

"Crimson Heart" is a classic coming-of-age story of girls. The story takes place in Refugia, a controlled city completely cut off from the magic of Unknown, a magical land overrun by the Blue Fireflies. The story unfolds as the girls who have been managed through a perfect system in Refugia one day find a mysterious old book and a red necklace in the library, and go on an adventure to a strange world in search of "The Island of Blue Fireflies".
