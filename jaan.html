Jaan (이안)

He is one of the main characters of Dark Moon: The Blood Altar.
He is a vampire in the twelfth grade at Decelis Academy and the enforcer of the Nightball Team.

Personality

Jaan is as solid as a rock. Compared to his brothers, he can be a little closed off and blunt, but he cares about his brothers with all his heart. He is thoughtful and considerate deep down.
