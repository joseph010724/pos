Park Sung-hoon (Hangul: 박성훈, Japanese: パク・ソンフン)

Personal Information

More well known by his stage name Sunghoon (Hangul: 성훈, Japanese: ソンフン)

Is a member of the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. He was born on December 8, 2002, and is the visual of the group.

Career

2010-2019: Figure skater Sunghoon won two international advanced novice competitions, Asian Figure Skating Trophy and Lombardia Trophy, during the 2015-16 figure skating season. Sunghoon made his international junior debut during the 2016-17 figure skating season when he became age eligible. He earned a silver medal at Asian Figure Skating Trophy and participated in ISU Junior Grand Prix in Estonia as a representative of South Korea. Next year, he won another silver medal at Asian Figure Skating Trophy and participated in ISU Junior Grand Prix in Poland. During the 2018-19 figure skating season, Sung-hoon became age-eligible for senior competitions. He participated in two ISU Challenger Series events, Asian Figure Skating Trophy and Alpen Trophy. 2020: I-LAND On June 1, 2020, Sunghoon, along with trainees Sunoo, Jake, Heeseung, K, and Daniel, via Twitter was revealed as an I-LAND contestant. It was also revealed he had been a trainee for two years and one month. On September 18, 2020, he ranked 6th place, becoming a part of the official lineup of ENHYPEN. I-LAND Missions Main Article: I-LAND Part One Entry Test For the entry test, Sunghoon performed "The 7th Sense" by NCT U, along with Jay. He received a score of 21/22 (95.5%), therefore passing into I-LAND. Theme Song Sunghoon had Part 7 position. He stays in I-LAND. Teamwork Sunghoon had Part 9 and stays in I-LAND. Representative Unit Originally Jay was in the dance unit, but he switched parts with Sunghoon. Sunghoon performed " Rainism, 3 Dope Boyz & One of a Kind" by Rain, Dynamic Duo, G-Dragon along with Jungwon and K and was in the dance unit. Fortunately, they received a score of 73, which was higher than the dance Grounders score, he stayed in I-LAND. The Final 12 Sunghoon is saved from being dropped to ground; thus, he'll continue on to the second part of I-LAND. Sunghoon had Part 3. Part Two BTS Test Sunghoon was in the "Fake Love" unit that ranked first. The 1st, 2nd, and 3rd rank will be spending a day of freedom outside I-Land doing different activities. Sunghoon received 3rd rank and was able to spend a day of freedom alongside Heeseung and Jungwon. Chemistry Test Sunghoon was in the "Flicker" team. Concept Test Sunghoon had Part 5 with the song "Chamber 5 (Dreams of Dreams)". Final Test On September 18, 2020, Sunghoon placed 6th, and became a member of the boy group ENHYPEN.



Park Jong-seong (Hangul: 박종성, Japanese: パク・ジョンソン)

More well known by his stage name Jay (Hangul: 제이, Japanese: ジェイ)

Is a member of the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. He was born on April 20, 2002.

Career

2020: I-LAND On June 2, 2020, Jay, along with trainees Geonu, EJ, Jungwon, Kyungmin, and Hanbin via Twitter was revealed as an I-LAND contestant. It was also revealed he had been a trainee for two years and eleven months. On September 18, 2020, he ranked 2nd place, becoming a part of the official lineup of ENHYPEN. I-LAND Missions Main Article: I-LAND Part One Entry Test For the entry test, Jay performed "The 7th Sense" by NCT U, along with Sunghoon. He received a score of 19/22 (86.4%), therefore passing into I-LAND. Theme Song Jay had Part 8 position. He leaves I-LAND and goes to Ground. Teamwork Jay had Part 1 (Center) and moved from Ground to I-LAND. Jay and NI-KI became the leaders of the Grounders. Representative Unit Jay originally was in the dance unit, but he switched parts with Sunghoon. Fortunately, they received a score of 73, which was higher than the dance Grounders' score, he stayed in I-LAND. The Final 12 Jay is saved from being dropped to ground, thus, he'll continue on to the second part of I-LAND. Jay had Part 6. Part Two BTS Test Jay was in the "DNA" unit that ranked second. Chemistry Test Jay was in the "Dive Into You" team. Concept Test Jay had Part 5 with the song "Flame On". Final Test On September 18, 2020, Jay placed 2nd, and became a member of the boy group ENHYPEN.

Sim Jae-yun (Hangul: 심재윤, Japanese: シム・ジェユン)

More well known by his stage name Jake (Hangul: 제이크, Japanese: ジェイク)

Is a member of the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. He was born on November 15, 2002.

Career

2020: I-LAND On June 1, 2020, Jake, along with trainees Sunoo, Sunghoon, Heeseung, K, and Daniel, via Twitter was revealed as an I-LAND contestant. It was also revealed he had been a trainee for nine months. On September 18, 2020, he ranked 3rd place, becoming a part of the official lineup of ENHYPEN. I-LAND Missions Main Article: I-LAND Part One Entry Test For the entry test, Jake performed "Crown" by TXT, along with Sunoo and Youngbin. He received a score of 12/22 (54.5%), passing into I-LAND. Theme Song Jake had Part 11. The producers gave him a score of 40, the lowest score from amongst the I-landers. Ultimately, he was voted out of I-LAND and was one of the six participants to be removed to Ground. Unfortunately, a technical difficulty with the mic resulted in Jake not being able to sing his part. Teamwork Jake had Part 5 and was moved from Ground to I-LAND alongside four other Grounders. Representative Unit Jake was not a representative for any of the units. He stayed in I-LAND. The Final 12 Jake is saved from being dropped to ground; thus, he continued on to Part 2 of I-LAND. Jake had Part 8. Part Two BTS Test Jake was in the "I Need U" unit, which ranked last of the three units. Chemistry Test Jake was in the "Flicker" team. Jake was picked to be ranked first by the producers, and the votes he received in the last 24 hours of voting were doubled. Concept Test Jake had Part 4 with the song "Chamber 5 (Dreams of Dreams)". Final Test On September 18, 2020, Jake placed 3rd, and became a member of the boy group ENHYPEN.



Kim Sun-woo (Hangul: 김선우, Japanese: キム・ソヌ)

Personal Information

More well known by his stage name Sunoo (Hangul: 선우, Japanese: ソヌ)

Is a member of the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. He was born on June 24, 2003.

Career

2020: I-LAND On June 1, 2020, Sunoo, along with trainees Jake, Sunghoon, Heeseung, K, and Daniel, via Twitter was revealed as an I-LAND contestant. It was also revealed he had been a trainee for ten months. On September 18, 2020, he ranked 8th place, but he was chosen by the producers, becoming a part of the official lineup of ENHYPEN. I-LAND Missions Main Article: I-LAND Part One Entry Test For the entry test, Sunoo performed "Crown" by TXT, along with Jake and Youngbin. He received a score of 20/22 (90.9%), therefore passing into I-LAND. However, he was later voted to be moved to Ground. Theme Song For this test, the Grounders did not perform. Teamwork For the Teamwork test, Sunoo was Part 6, but stayed in Ground. Representative Unit Sunoo performed "Save Me" by BTS along with Daniel, and was in the vocal unit. Unfortunately, because they received a score of 74, which was less than the vocal I-LANDers score, he stayed in Ground. The Final 12 Sunoo was still in ground. Part Two BTS Test Sunoo was in the "Fake Love" unit that ranked first. Chemistry Test Sunoo was in the "Dive Into You" team. Concept Test Sunoo won first place and will pick his chosen song and members. Sunoo had Part 2 with the song "Chamber 5 (Dreams of Dreams)". Final Test On September 18, 2020, Sunoo placed 8th, but he was chosen by the producers to debut and became the last member of the boy group ENHYPEN.

He leaves I-LAND and goes to Ground. Teamwork For the Teamwork test, NI-KI had Part 2, but stayed in Ground. NI-KI and Jay became the leaders of the Grounders. Representative Unit NI-KI performed "Warrior's Descendant, Reversal & One of a Kind" by H.O.T., X-Teen & G-Dragon along with Nicholas and Jaeho, and was in the dance unit. Unfortunately, they received a score of 65, which was less than the dance I-LANDers score, he stayed in Ground. The Final 12 NI-KI was still in ground.
