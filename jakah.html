Jakah (자카)

He is one of the main characters of Dark Moon: The Blood Altar.
He is a vampire at Decelis Academy and the wing of the Nightball Team.

Personality

Jakah is the smartest among his vampire brothers. He's quick on his feet and knows how to make rational decisions. The brothers have been through a lot living among humans while hiding their identities, and he believes that he has to do better and step up to protect his brothers.

Powers and Abilities

• Vampire Physiology: As a Vampire, Jakah possesses superhuman agility, durability, strength, speed, stamina and reflexes, also immortality and psychic communication.
• Superhuman Agility: As a Vampire, Jakah has a heightened level of agillity in his movements.
• Superhuman Durability: As a Vampire, Jakah has heightened amounts of durability.
• Superhuman Strength: As a Vampire, Jakah is incredibly strong.
• Superhuman Stamina: As a Vampire, Jakah possesses an increased stamina.
• Superhuman Reflexes: As a Vampire, Jakah has superhuman reflexes.
• Immortality: As a Vampire, Jakah is genetically able to live forever, without dying due to old age or illnesses.
• Psychic Communication
• Supernatural Speed
