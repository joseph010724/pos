Shion (시온)

He is one of the main characters of Dark Moon: The Blood Altar.
He is a vampire in the tenth grade at Decelis Academy and a back of the Nightball Team.

Personality

JShion has the most friends at school, and he's a troublemaker who does his own thing. He's very honest with his feelings and straightforward. He doesn't care whether his brothers think it's complicated or not. If he wants to be friends with the new kid in school, Sooha, he will be!

Powers and Abilities

• Vampire Physiology: As a Vampire, Shion possesses superhuman agility, durability, strength, speed, stamina and reflexes, also immortality and psychic communication.
• Superhuman Agility: As a Vampire, Shion has a heightened level of agillity in his movements.
• Superhuman Durability: As a Vampire, Shion has heightened amounts of durability.
• Superhuman Strength: As a Vampire, Shion is incredibly strong.
• Superhuman Speed: As a Vampire, Shion posesses heightened levels of speed.
• Superhuman Stamina: As a Vampire, Shion possesses an increased stamina.
• Superhuman Reflexes: As a Vampire, Shion has superhuman reflexes.
• Immortality: As a Vampire, Shion is genetically able to live forever, without dying due to old age or illnesses.
