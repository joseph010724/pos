Jino (지노)

He is one of the main characters of Dark Moon: The Blood Altar.
He is a vampire in the eleventh grade at Decelis Academy and a back of the Nightball Team.

Personality

Jino is upbeat and candid about his feelings. He doesn't care whether the person in front of him is a human or a vampire. He does really well in school and makes a lot of good memories with his human friends.

Powers and Abilities

• Vampire Physiology: As a Vampire, Jino possesses superhuman agility, durability, strength, speed, stamina and reflexes, also immortality and psychic communication.
• Superhuman Agility: As a Vampire, Jino has a heightened level of agility in his movements.
• Superhuman Durability: As a Vampire, Jino has heightened amounts of durability.
• Superhuman Strength: As a Vampire, Jino is incredibly strong.
• Superhuman Speed: As a Vampire, Jino posesses heightened levels of speed.
• Superhuman Stamina: As a Vampire, Jino possesses an increased stamina.
• Superhuman Reflexes: As a Vampire, Jino has superhuman reflexes.
• Immortality: As a Vampire, Jino is genetically able to live forever, without dying due to old age or illnesses.
