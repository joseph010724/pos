Noa (노아)

He is one of the main characters of Dark Moon: The Blood Altar.
He is a vampire at Decelis Academy and a forward of the Nightball Team.

Personality

Noa is a guy that has a lot of potential. He can control darkness so there's nothing he can't do as long as he makes good use of his power. He's the youngest of the vampire brothers, and he wants to be as cool as them, but tends to be a little hasty because of it.

Powers and Abilities

• Vampire Physiology: As a Vampire, Noa possesses superhuman agility, durability, strength, speed, stamina and reflexes, also immortality and psychic communication.
• Superhuman Agility: As a Vampire, Noa has a heightened level of agillity in his movements.
• Superhuman Durability: As a Vampire, Noa has heightened amounts of durability.
• Superhuman Strength: As a Vampire, Noa is incredibly strong.
• Superhuman Speed: As a Vampire, Noa posesses heightened levels of speed.
• Superhuman Stamina: As a Vampire, Noa possesses an increased stamina.
• Superhuman Reflexes: As a Vampire, Noa has superhuman reflexes.
• Immortality: As a Vampire, Noa is genetically able to live forever, without dying due to old age or illnesses.
• Psychic Communication
• Darkness Manipulation
